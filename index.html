Variance Risk Premium Strategy

January 2025

Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib yfinance

Investigated the variance risk premium phenomenon using 15 years of S&P 500 and VIX data. Built a proxy framework to simulate short-variance exposure and tested whether timing mechanisms add value beyond passive exposure to the premium.

Key Findings

  • Demonstrated VRP exists consistently: implied variance exceeded realized variance in majority of periods
  • Debugged critical backtesting errors (lookahead bias, incorrect annualization) that inflated initial Sharpe ratio
  • Found timing mechanisms primarily affected risk profile rather than returns; always-on exposure captured ~90% of total gains
  • Documented limitations of proxy-based approach without access to actual variance swap curves

Portfolio Optimization Analysis

November 2024 - December 2024

Python Pandas NumPy SciPy yfinance

Tested whether mean-variance optimization can produce repeatable out-of-sample edge. Implemented Sharpe ratio maximization on a diversified ETF basket, comparing daily-rebalanced vs buy-and-hold approaches across train/test splits.

Key Findings

  • Optimized portfolio achieved 3.04% outperformance vs S&P 500 in-sample (2018-2021)
  • Out-of-sample performance degraded significantly due to mean reversion and correlation shifts
  • Diagnosed overfitting through regime analysis: parameter estimates unstable across periods
  • Demonstrated importance of walk-forward testing and realistic expectations for mean-variance approaches
